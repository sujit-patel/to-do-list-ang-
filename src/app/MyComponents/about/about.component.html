About This App

Welcome to the To-Do-List app — your personal productivity partner built with Angular and Bootstrap 5. This app helps you efficiently track, manage, and organize your daily tasks.

Features:

  • ✅ Add new tasks
  • ✅ Delete completed or unwanted tasks
  • ✅ Data saved in browser's local storage
  • ✅ Clean, responsive UI with Bootstrap 5
